Hometown Automatic Car Washes

Tableau skills project

I was curious about how automatic car washes in my hometown of Owensboro compared to each other. Both in terms of price and features. I collected some data from their websites where availble, and I visited the ones that didn't have websites with this information. Since there are only 13 car washes in Owensboro, then going to each location to verify information wasn't that difficult. After collecting the data, I wanted to vizualize it on a map. I used Tableau Public to create the dashboard and you can click on the picture to take you to the interactive version on Tableau's website.
